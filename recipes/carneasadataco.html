Carne Asada Taco

Delicious and tenderness, my personal favorite.

All you need is a handful of ingredients for this recipe and ideally a hot outdoor grill. Though an indoor grill pan works just fine for the carne asada as well!

Ingredients

  • Carne Asada
  • Avocado
  • Lime
  • Cilantro
  • Cheese
  • Onion
  • Tortillas

Steps

  1. Grab a tortilla and slather it with some avocado.
  2. Add a good helping of chopped carne asada.
  3. A sprinle of cotija or queso fresco cheese.
  4. Chopped inion and fresh cilantro.
  5. Squeeze some fresh lime on top.
