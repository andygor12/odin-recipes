Instant Noodle

Description

My way of making instant noodles!

Ingredients

  • Any instant noodle brands
  • 1 Egg
  • Green Scallions
  • Vienna Sausage

Steps

  1. Add 500ml of water into a cooking pot.
  2. Add noodles to the cooking pot.
  3. Set to high heat and cover the cooking pot.
  4. Remove lid when it starts to boil.
  5. Add egg and seasoning packets into the pot.
  6. Stir until the egg is scrambled.
  7. Chop some green scallions and put into pot.
  8. Add vienna sausages into pot.
  9. Pour out the finished dish into a bowl and enjoy!
